Minecraft Skin Viewer

Look up any Minecraft player's current skin and cape, rendered in an interactive 3D viewer.

Instructions

Enter a Minecraft Java Edition username and click "Load" (or press Enter) to fetch that player's current skin and cape.

Drag the model with your mouse (or finger, on touch devices) to rotate it, and scroll (or pinch, on touch devices) to zoom. Use Pause/Play to toggle the walking animation.

This looks up the player's current skin directly from Mojang, so it always reflects whatever they're wearing right now.


Flat image renders, powered by VZGE.
