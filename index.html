Who I Am

I describe myself as a college student, programmer and world-builder crafting websites, tools, and stories for Minecraft communities.

I split my time between writing code and writing lore. On the technical side, that means building small tools that save creators time, like a book converter, a Discord timestamp generator, and several Discord bots.
